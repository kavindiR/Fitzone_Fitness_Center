Support & FAQs

Find answers to the most frequently asked questions about FitZone Fitness Center.

What are your membership cancellation policies?

You can cancel your membership at any time. For monthly plans, cancellation is effective at the end of your billing cycle. For yearly plans, refunds are not available after the first 30 days.

Can I pause my membership?

Yes, you can pause your membership for up to 2 months per year due to medical or travel reasons. Reach out to our front desk to request a freeze.

Do I need to book classes in advance?

We recommend booking group classes in advance through our mobile app or website to secure your spot. Walk-ins are welcome based on availability.

Do you offer personal training?

Yes! We have certified personal trainers available for both one-on-one and small group training sessions. You can add personal training to any plan.

Are there any discounts for students?

Absolutely. Students with valid ID receive 15% off on all monthly plans.

How do I contact support?

You can reach us through the Contact Page or email support@fitzone.lk. Our response time is usually under 24 hours.
